Post-Eady is a collective formed in 2016 after attending the Royal College of Art as a follow up to the graphic design named after Royal College of Art’s student platform ‘Eady Forum’. It is intended as a space for workshop creation and critical discussion around graphic design. The current members are Vilja Achté, Joana Pestana & Max Ryan.
